Introduction

This category, PHY 162a Problem Sets, contains my personal notes and solutions for the Quantum Mechanics course at Brandeis University.
You may use these notes as a reference. If you notice any mistakes, please feel free to contact me at suwako2524@gmail.com.

I recommend Курс теоретической физики (Course of Theoretical Physics) by Landau and Lifshitz for reviewing Quantum Mechanics.
However, this collection is not suitable for beginners who are learning Quantum Mechanics for the first time.
