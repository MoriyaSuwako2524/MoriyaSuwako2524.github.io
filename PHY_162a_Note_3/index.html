1 dimension motion

Which means the motion can only happen in position that E>=U . In another word, The potential point that equal to total energy determines the boundary of motion.
The point that E=U means velocity equals to 0.

Since the velocity at these points is zero, they are called turning points.
If the region of motion is bounded by two turning points, the motion occurs within a finite spatial region and is called bounded motion.
If the region of motion is unbounded or limited only on one side, the motion is unbounded, and the particle can move infinitely far away, this is called unbounded motion.
